Hi, I am a Game Programming student enrolled at Bournemouth University. I have been interested in computers and technology ever since I was a kid, and have been developing my skills ever since. I've been gaming about as long, starting on the Mega Drive and moving onto PC later on. My favourite genres include: Strategy, RPG and Sandbox.

My first experiences of programming were modifying games or just opening them up to see how they functioned.

I have always enjoyed learning about new technologies and how I can use them to keep adapting my skills with the latest information.
